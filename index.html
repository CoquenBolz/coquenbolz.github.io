Information on HomeOwner's Insurance

HOI Basic Coverage includes

  • damage from fire
  • damage from lightning
  • damage from wind
  • damage from hail
  • damage from smoke
  • damage from volcanic eruption
  • damage from explosion
  • damage from riot/civil unrest

HOI Broad Coverage includes

  • that of Basic Coverage
  • damage from building collapse
  • damage to heating
  • damage to air-con
  • damage to plumbing
  • damage to sprinkler systems
  • damage from sudden tearing/cracking/burning
  • damage from electronics/falling objects/weight of ice

HOI Special Form All Risk Coverage includes

  • that of Basic and Broad Coverage
  • damage from sudden tearing/cracking/burning
  • Loss of use covered under this policy
  • Covers all physical damage or loss except those excluded
